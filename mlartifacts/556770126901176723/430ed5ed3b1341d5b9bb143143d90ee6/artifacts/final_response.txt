No, Air India does not allow a name change on a PNR once it has been created by a travel agent. Once the booking is made, the name elements must match the travel document (Passport, Approved government ID) and changes to the name field are not permitted. If there are misspellings, you should contact Air India Reservation offices for assistance, as they may consider requests on a case-by-case basis, but the maximum number of characters that can be changed is limited to three. Additionally, name changes must be made at least 72 hours before the departure time.
