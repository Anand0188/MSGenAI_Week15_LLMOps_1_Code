The Department of Transportation (DOT) enforces the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) specifically with respect to airlines. This means that DOT is responsible for ensuring that airlines do not obtain personal information from children under the age of 13 without first obtaining parental consent. The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) shares jurisdiction over ticket agents concerning COPPA regulations. If you need further information about COPPA, you can visit the FTC COPPA Page for more details.
