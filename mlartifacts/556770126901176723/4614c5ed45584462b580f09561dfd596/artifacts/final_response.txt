According to Air India's waiver policy, a domestic flight must be rescheduled for more than 1 hour to qualify for ticket reissuance without change charges. However, please note that the policy specifies that refunds are not required when the reason for the cancellation or delay is outside of the carrier's control. Therefore, if you're looking for a full refund specifically due to a reschedule, the policy may not support that unless additional criteria are met.
