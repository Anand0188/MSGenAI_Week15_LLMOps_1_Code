For an international flight with a duration of 14 hours, the delay threshold for a mandatory baggage fee refund under Department of Transportation (DOT) rules is 30 hours. This applies because the flight segment between the United States and the foreign point is over 12 hours. Therefore, if the airline fails to deliver the checked bag within 30 hours of the passenger's flight arriving at the gate, and the passenger has filed a Mishandled Baggage Report, the airline is required to refund the baggage fee.
