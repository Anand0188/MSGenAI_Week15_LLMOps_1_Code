For an international flight with a duration of 14 hours, the delay threshold for a mandatory baggage fee refund is 30 hours. According to the U.S. Department of Transportation rules, since the flight duration is over 12 hours, the airline must deliver the delayed bag within 30 hours of the passenger's flight arriving at the gate in order to avoid refunding the baggage fee. If the bag is not delivered within that timeframe, and the passenger has filed a Mishandled Baggage Report, they should be eligible for a refund of the baggage fee.
