No, Air India does not allow a name change on a PNR once it has been created by a travel agent. Once the booking is made, the name elements provided must match the travel document, and modifications to the name field are not permitted. If there is a misspelling, it is advised to contact Air India Reservation offices for assistance, as they may consider requests on a case-by-case basis, but name changes must be made at least 72 hours before the departure time.
