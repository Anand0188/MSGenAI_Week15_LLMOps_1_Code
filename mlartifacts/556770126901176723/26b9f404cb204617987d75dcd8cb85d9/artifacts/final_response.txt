For an international flight with a duration of 14 hours, the delay threshold for a mandatory baggage fee refund is 30 hours. According to the Department of Transportation (DOT) rules, if the flight segment between the United States and a foreign point is over 12 hours, the airline must deliver the delayed bag within 30 hours of the passenger's flight arriving at the gate for the passenger to be eligible for a refund of the baggage fee, provided that a Mishandled Baggage Report has been filed.
