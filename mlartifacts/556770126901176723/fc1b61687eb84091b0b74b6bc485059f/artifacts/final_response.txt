No, Air India does not allow a name change on a PNR once it has been created by a travel agent. Once the booking is made, the name elements must match the travel document (like a passport or approved government ID), and changing the name field is not permitted. If there is a misspelling, you should contact Air India Reservation offices for assistance, as they may consider the request on a case-by-case basis, allowing a maximum change of three characters. It's important to note that any name changes must be made at least 72 hours before the departure time.
