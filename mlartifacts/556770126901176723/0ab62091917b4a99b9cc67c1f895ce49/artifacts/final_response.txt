The Department of Transportation (DOT) enforces the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) specifically with respect to airlines. This means that DOT takes action to ensure that airlines comply with COPPA regulations, which prohibit operators of websites from obtaining personal information from children under the age of 13 without first obtaining parental consent. For ticket agents, both DOT and the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) share jurisdiction regarding COPPA compliance.

If you need further information about COPPA or its enforcement, you can visit the FTC COPPA Page for additional resources. If you have more specific questions or concerns, feel free to ask!
