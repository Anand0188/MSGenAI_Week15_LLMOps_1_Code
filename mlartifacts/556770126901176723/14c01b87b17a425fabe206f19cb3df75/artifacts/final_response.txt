The Department of Transportation (DOT) enforces the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) specifically with respect to airlines. This means that airlines must comply with COPPA regulations when it comes to obtaining personal information from children under the age of 13, which requires obtaining parental consent before collecting such information. 

Additionally, the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) shares jurisdiction over ticket agents regarding COPPA compliance.
