No, Air India does not allow a name change on a PNR once it has been created by a travel agent. The name elements provided must match the travel document (such as a passport or approved government ID), and changing the name field after the PNR is created is not permitted. If there is a misspelling, it is advised to contact Air India Reservation offices for assistance, as they may consider requests on a case-by-case basis, but this must be done at least 72 hours before the departure time.
