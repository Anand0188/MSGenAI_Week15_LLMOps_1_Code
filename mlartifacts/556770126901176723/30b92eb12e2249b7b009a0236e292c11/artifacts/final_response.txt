If an airline files for bankruptcy, you must file a claim with your credit card company within 60 days after the date you received the first monthly statement that listed the charge for the transportation. It's important to act quickly and gather the necessary documentation to support your claim, such as your account number, a photocopy of the ticket or receipt, and a statement indicating that the airline has ceased operations and you did not receive the service you paid for. 

If you have any further questions or need assistance with the claims process, feel free to ask!
