For a 'Churn Violation' in Economy Class for Air India bookings, if the allowed limit of 4 churns is exceeded, a penalty of US$ 10 will be charged for each additional churn done per passenger per segment. This means that if you make more than 4 bookings for the same passenger on the same route, you will incur this fee for each additional booking beyond the threshold.
