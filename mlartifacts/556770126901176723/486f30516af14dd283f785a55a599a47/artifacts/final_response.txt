The penalty for a 'Churn Violation' in Economy Class for Air India bookings is US$10 for each additional churn beyond the allowed limit of 4. This means if a travel agent makes more than 4 bookings for the same passenger on the same route, they will incur a fee of US$10 for each additional booking (churn) per passenger per segment.
