No, Air India does not allow a name change on a PNR once it has been created by a travel agent. Once the booking is made, the name fields cannot be modified. If there are misspellings or incorrect names, travel agents must contact Air India Reservation offices for assistance. Air India may consider requests for corrections on a case-by-case basis, but the maximum number of characters that can be changed is limited to three. Additionally, any name changes must be made at least 72 hours before the departure time.
