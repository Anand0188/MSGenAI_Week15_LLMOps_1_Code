The Department of Transportation (DOT) enforces the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) specifically with respect to airlines. This means that DOT ensures compliance with COPPA regulations, which prohibit operators of websites from obtaining personal information from children under the age of 13 without first obtaining parental consent. 

For ticket agents, the enforcement of COPPA is shared between the DOT and the Federal Trade Commission (FTC). If you need further information about COPPA, you can visit the FTC’s COPPA page. If you have more specific questions or need assistance with a particular situation, please let me know!
