The Department of Transportation (DOT) enforces the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) specifically with respect to airlines. This means that airlines are prohibited from obtaining personal information from children under the age of 13 without first obtaining parental consent. 

For ticket agents, both the DOT and the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) share jurisdiction regarding COPPA compliance. If you have further questions about COPPA or need more details, you may want to visit the FTC's COPPA page for additional information.
