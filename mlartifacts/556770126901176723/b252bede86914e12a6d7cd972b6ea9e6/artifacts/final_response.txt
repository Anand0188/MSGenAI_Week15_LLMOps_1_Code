Air India does not allow a name change on a PNR once it has been created by a travel agent. Changing the name field after the PNR is created is not permitted. If there is a misspelling or error in the name, it is recommended to contact Air India Reservation offices for assistance, as they may consider requests on a case-by-case basis. However, the changes are limited to a maximum of three characters, and supporting documents may be required. Additionally, any name changes must be made at least 72 hours before the departure time.

If you need to make a correction after ticketing, a new PNR must be created with the correct name, and a new ticket must be issued. Please ensure that the original itinerary matches exactly and follow the specific procedures outlined by Air India for such corrections.
