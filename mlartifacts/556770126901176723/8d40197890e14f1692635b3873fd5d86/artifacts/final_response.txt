The penalty for a 'Churn Violation' in Economy Class for Air India bookings is US$10 for each additional churn that exceeds the allowed limit of 4 bookings per passenger per segment. If an agent makes more than 4 bookings for the same passenger on the same route, they will incur this penalty for each additional booking beyond the limit.
