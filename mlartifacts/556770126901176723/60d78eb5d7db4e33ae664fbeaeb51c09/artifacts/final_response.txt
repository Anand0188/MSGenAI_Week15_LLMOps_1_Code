For an international flight with a duration of 14 hours, the delay threshold for a mandatory baggage fee refund under DOT rules is 30 hours. This applies because the flight segment between the United States and the foreign point is over 12 hours. Therefore, if the airline fails to deliver the checked bag within 30 hours after the passenger's flight has arrived at the gate, the passenger is entitled to a refund of the baggage fee, provided they have filed a Mishandled Baggage Report.
