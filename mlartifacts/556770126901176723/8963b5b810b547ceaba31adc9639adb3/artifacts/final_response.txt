No, Air India does not allow a name change on a PNR once it has been created. The name elements provided in the reservation must match the travel document (Passport or Approved government ID), and changing the name field after the PNR is created is not permitted. If there is a misspelling or error in the name, you must contact Air India Reservation offices for assistance, as they may consider requests on a case-by-case basis. However, note that the maximum number of characters that can be changed is limited to three (3), and changes must be made at least 72 hours before departure.
