No, Air India does not allow a name change on a PNR once it has been created. Once the booking is confirmed, changes to the name field are not permitted. If there is a misspelling or error in the name, it is advised to contact Air India Reservation offices for assistance, as they may consider requests on a case-by-case basis, but only for minor corrections (up to three characters). Additionally, name changes must be requested at least 72 hours before the departure time.
