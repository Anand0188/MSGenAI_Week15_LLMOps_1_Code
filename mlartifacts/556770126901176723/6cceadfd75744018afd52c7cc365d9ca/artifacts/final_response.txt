Yes, a ticket agent can retain a service fee if a flight is canceled and the passenger is entitled to a refund. However, there are specific conditions that must be met:

1. The service fee must be for services beyond merely processing the payment for the flight that the consumer found.
2. The fee must be disclosed clearly and prominently at the time of ticket purchase, including its nature, amount, and whether it is refundable.
3. The fee retention must be on a per-passenger basis.

If these conditions are satisfied, the ticket agent may retain the service fee even if the passenger did not travel due to the airline's cancellation. If you have further questions or need assistance with a specific situation, feel free to ask!
