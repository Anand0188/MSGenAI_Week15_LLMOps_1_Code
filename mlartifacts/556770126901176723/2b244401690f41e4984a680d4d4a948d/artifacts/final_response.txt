Yes, according to the Department of Transportation (DOT) privacy guidelines, it is considered a 'deceptive' practice for an airline to mislead a consumer regarding a material matter. A practice is deemed deceptive if it is likely to mislead a consumer acting reasonably under the circumstances about a material issue. A material matter is one that is likely to affect the consumer's conduct or decision concerning a product or service. Therefore, if an airline provides misleading information that could influence a consumer's choices, it may be in violation of these guidelines.
