For an international flight with a duration of 14 hours, the delay threshold for a mandatory baggage fee refund, according to the Department of Transportation (DOT) rules, is 30 hours. This is because the flight duration exceeds 12 hours, allowing airlines to have up to 30 hours to deliver the delayed baggage before a refund of the baggage fee is required, provided that the passenger has filed a Mishandled Baggage Report.
