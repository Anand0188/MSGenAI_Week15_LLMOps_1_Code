Yes, according to the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) guidelines, it is considered a 'deceptive' practice for an airline to mislead a consumer regarding a material matter. A practice is deemed 'deceptive' if it is likely to mislead a consumer, acting reasonably under the circumstances, about a material matter. A material matter is one that is likely to affect the consumer's conduct or decision regarding a product or service. Therefore, if an airline fails to disclose important information or provides misleading information, it could be classified as deceptive under DOT regulations.
