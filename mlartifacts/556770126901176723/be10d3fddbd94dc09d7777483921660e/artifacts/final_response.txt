According to Air India's waiver policy, a domestic flight that is rescheduled for more than 1 hour qualifies for a full refund. To process this refund, you will need to insert the following remark in the booking PNR: "OSI: AI CANCEL / AI ### / DDMMYY," where "AI ###" is the flight number and "DDMMYY" is the date of cancellation. Additionally, the entire booking must be cancelled to avail of the full refund. If you have any further questions or need assistance with the process, feel free to ask!
