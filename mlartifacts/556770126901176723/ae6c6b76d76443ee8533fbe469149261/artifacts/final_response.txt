The Department of Transportation (DOT) enforces the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) specifically with respect to airlines. This means that airlines must comply with COPPA regulations when collecting personal information from children under the age of 13. The Federal Trade Commission (FTC) shares jurisdiction over ticket agents regarding COPPA compliance. If you have further questions about COPPA or its enforcement, you can refer to the FTC COPPA Page for more detailed information.
