No, Air India does not allow a name change on a PNR once it has been created by a travel agent. According to Air India's policies, changing the name field is not permitted after the PNR is created. If there is a misspelling, travel agents should contact Air India's Reservation offices for assistance, but name changes should be made at least 72 hours before the departure time and are considered on a case-by-case basis. 

If you need to change a name after ticketing, specific procedures must be followed, including creating a new PNR with the correct name and issuing a new ticket. Please ensure that the request is made within the guidelines provided.
