A travel voucher issued to a passenger who cannot fly due to a serious communicable disease must have the following mandatory characteristics:

1. **Transferrable**: The voucher must be able to be transferred to another person.

2. **Validity Period**: The voucher must be valid for at least 5 years from the date of issuance.

Additionally, passengers requesting such a voucher may need to provide documentary evidence demonstrating their inability to travel or that they have been advised not to travel due to health concerns related to the communicable disease.
