For an international flight with a duration of 14 hours, the delay threshold for a mandatory baggage fee refund under Department of Transportation (DOT) rules is 30 hours. This applies because the flight duration is over 12 hours, which allows carriers to recover delayed bags within this longer timeframe. If the airline fails to deliver the checked bag within 30 hours of the flight's arrival at the gate, and the passenger has filed a Mishandled Baggage Report, they would be eligible for a refund of the baggage fee.
