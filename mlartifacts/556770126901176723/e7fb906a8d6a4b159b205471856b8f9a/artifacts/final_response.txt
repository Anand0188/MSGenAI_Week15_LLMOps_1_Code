According to Air India's waiver policy, for a domestic flight (not connected with an international flight) to qualify for a full refund due to rescheduling, the flight must be rescheduled for more than 1 hour. If this condition is met, cancellation charges are waived, and you can process a full refund. 

Please ensure that the entire booking is canceled to avail of the full refund. If you need further assistance or have more specific questions about your booking, feel free to ask!
